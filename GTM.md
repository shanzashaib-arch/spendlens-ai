# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Target User

The primary target user is a technical founder or engineering manager at a seed-stage or Series A startup with 5–50 employees actively paying for multiple AI productivity tools.

## Discovery Moments

Potential users are likely to search for:
- “reduce ChatGPT team costs”
- “best AI coding tools for startups”
- “Claude vs ChatGPT pricing”
- “AI SaaS cost optimization”

## Online Communities

Target communities include:
- r/startups
- Indie Hackers
- Hacker News
- AI engineering Discord communities
- SaaS founder Twitter/X circles

## First 100 Users

I would:
- post audit examples publicly on X
- share case-study style breakdowns
- launch on Product Hunt
- create short comparison content
- DM founders with personalized audit insights

## Unfair Distribution Advantage

Credex already operates in the AI infrastructure ecosystem, which provides credibility and existing relationships with AI-heavy startups.
